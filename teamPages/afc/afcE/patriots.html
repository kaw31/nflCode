The New England Patriots are based in the greater Boston region in the state of Massachusetts. They were founded in 1959, and were an original member of the American Football League (AFL), who joined the NFL in the 1970 merger. The team changed its name from the original Boston Patriots after relocating to Foxborough in 1971. They now compete in the AFC East Division, and have a fierce rivalry with the New York Jets.

The Patriots are the reigning Super Bowl Champions, having pulled off the greatest comeback in NFL history against the Atlanta Falcons in Superbowl LI, by overcoming a 25 point difference. They have become the most feared team under the guidance of Head Coach Bill Belichick and Quarterback Tom Brady, having won 5 Super Bowls since 2002, becoming what many consider to be the greatest dynasty in the modern era of the NFL.

Roster

Head Coach: Bill Belichick

The Patriots are the most feared team in the league. But they'll only flirt with another perfect regular season. New England's roster has been upgraded since its Super Bowl LI victory. Brandin Cooks, Dwayne Allen and Stephon Gilmore are all welcome additions. However, the Patriots have areas of concern just like every other team. Their ability to generate pressure is in question. The offensive line performed well last season, but Tom Brady's protection remains of the utmost importance. Bill Belichick's squad is the favorite to win yet another championship, but it is far from perfect.

OFFENCE

There is a ton of talent on both sides of the ball, but especially on offence. The Pats have one of the highest scoring offenses in the league, with star Quarterback Tom Brady at the helm of the ship. Brady has a luxury of weapons to choose from. Rob 'Gronk' Gronkowski remains the best tight end in the game, and is almost impossible to stop when he's healthy. The receiving group is as strong as any, with Julian Edelman, Brandin Cooks and Chris Hogan all amazing. Last season, the offense was ranked 4th in Yards per game, and 3rd in Points per game. The Patriots can score from anywhere on the field.

DEFENCE

The defence can't be underestimated either, as they manage to 'bend-not-break', rarely giving up many points. This was evident last season as they ranked 1st in Fewest Points allowed per game, and 8th in Fewest Yards allowed per game. Key linebacker Dont'a Hightower takes the reigns of the defence, whilst cornerback duo Malcolm Butler and Stephon Gilmore, plus safety Devin McCourty protect the secondary. The only issue this unit face is on the defensive line. New England doesn't have many recognised pass rush specialists. Trey Flowers seems like the only one who could make some noise.

MVP

Tom Brady, QB

Although he's now 40 years old, Tom Brady is still the face of the franchise for the Patriots, and probably also the entire NFL. He remains one of the best players in the league, and many believe him to be the greatest quarterback of all time, which is why he's commonly reffered to as the G.O.A.T. (Greatest of All Time). This was on show again last year, when he led the Pats and himself to a 5th Super Bowl ring. No other QB has had more success than Brady.

Age is just a number for last season's Super Bowl MVP, whose countless wins, yards and touchdowns continue to amaze. There seems to be no stopping him. No other QB has ever won 5 Super Bowl rings, and at this rate he may be adding a few more before he eventually retires and goes straight into the Hall of Fame.

Other notable players: TE - Rob Gronkowski. WR - Julian Edelman. WR - Brandin Cooks. WR - Chris Hogan. LB - Dont'a Hightower. CB - Malcolm Butler. CB - Stephon Gilmore. S - Devin McCourty.
